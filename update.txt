Land border status, requirements for passage, wait times. UAE/Oman, Israel/Egypt, Israel/Jordan, Iran/Armenia:  

UAE-Oman 

Open. Anecdotal accounts of around 1-2 hours of wait time at Hatta crossing; non-Omani taxis not allowed to pass. Subject to temporary closures due to overloading. Available information indicates that Omani passports and Omani residents can still access the country even in moments of temporary closure. 

UAE-Saudi Arabia 

Border currently open, no indication of delays 

Israel-Egypt 

Taba remains open 24/7 as of March 2; no indication of delays. 

Israel-Jordan 

Yitzhak Rabin BXP remains open 0800-2000, and for vehicles until 1900. 

All other border crossings reportedly closed based on official orders. 

Iran-Turkiye 

Esendere-Serow, Kapikoy-Razi, and Gurbulak-Bazargan crossings: Temporarily restricting passenger traffic between all three border posts. Turkiye is only accepting Turkish citizens and citizens of other countries while Iran is only letting in Iranian citizens.

Cargo is allowed to move between all three posts.

Staffing increased to facilitate flow of traffic and trade.

Iran-Armenia 

Agarak/Norduz BXP open 24/7. No reports of delays.
